How to Play

This game is really simple the players can draw and play cards until the imploding puppy card is drawn.

When the Imploding puppy card is drawn, one of two things happen:

  • If the player has a kickball card they can kick the puppy back into the deck.
  • If not, then the puppy implodes and they die.

If you have a skip card you can skip drawing a card this turn. This can be useful but make sure you are on the lookout for the extra kickballs in the deck.
